about our
Project »

This project aims to address the challenges of accurately diagnosing various types of skin cancer, which is a prevalent and complex medical issue. Leveraging Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), the study focuses on multi-class classification of cutaneous cancer using a diverse dataset of dermoscopic images. The custom CNN model is optimized to efficiently identify distinctive image features, and extensive experiments are conducted to evaluate its performance. The research also explores techniques for visualizing learned features and addresses challenges like class imbalance and data augmentation to enhance model robustness. Ultimately, the project introduces a CNN-based framework for multi-class skin cancer detection, emphasizing the potential of deep learning techniques in improving diagnostic accuracy for more effective patient care.
